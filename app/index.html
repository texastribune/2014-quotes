In year where the governor got indicted, candidates duked it out in races for every statewide political office, and Ebola came to Dallas, there were plenty of contenders for the list — even without the Legislature in session. But we’ve done our best to collect the most noteworthy quotes of 2014, straight from the mouths of Texas politicos, be they silver-tongued or tongue-tied. Click through our slideshow to revisit their acrobatic missteps, well-packed verbal punches, and occasional moments of eloquence over the last 12 months.

"You know how long that takes in Texas at 75 miles an hour? That's a particularly flat highway."

-U.S. 5th Circuit Court Judge Edith Jones, suggesting that quick highways mean traveling hundreds of miles from the Rio Grande Valley to obtain an abortion wouldn't be an "undue burden," during a hearing the federal appeals court held on a new Texas abortion law.
